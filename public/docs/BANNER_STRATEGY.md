# Europack Banner Strategy: Meaning-Aligned Identity

**Golden Rule**: Focus on the **packaging process**, not logistics. Every visual must immediately communicate: *"This is a packaging company."*

## 🟧 ONE MESSAGE = ONE VISUAL

### Banner 1: Hero Protection
- **Message**: "We Don’t Just Move Products. We Protect Them."
- **Visual Goal**: Workers actively packing machines.
- **Key Elements**: Plastic wrap, wooden crates, hands-on tool work.
- **Anti-Pattern**: No empty warehouses or generic container ports.

### Banner 2: Product Scale (Pallets)
- **Message**: "Engineered Pallets Built for Global Load."
- **Visual Goal**: Mass scale pallet stacking.
- **Key Elements**: Clean wood textures, organized rows, scale.
- **Anti-Pattern**: No random crates or rusty materials.

### Banner 3: Export Readiness
- **Message**: "Export-Ready Packaging. Zero Risk Delivery."
- **Visual Goal**: Packed and labeled crates.
- **Key Elements**: "Fragile" stickers, "This Side Up" stencils, ready-to-ship crates.
- **Anti-Pattern**: No storage aisles without packaging context.

### Banner 4: Complete Solutions
- **Message**: "Complete Industrial Packaging Solutions Under One Roof."
- **Visual Goal**: Full packaging workflow.
- **Key Elements**: Vacuum wrapping + Crating + Lashing in one view.
- **Anti-Pattern**: No pure transport or shipping container shots.

---

## 🚀 Prompt Engineering Standards

Every image prompt generated for this ecosystem must include:
> *"Focus on packaging process, show workers handling packaging materials, avoid empty industrial or transport-only scenes, high-end cinematic lighting, ultra-detailed 8k realism."*
